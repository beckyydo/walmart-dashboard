Monthly Sales Across Years



Summary

• Built using d3 in javascript. Data-bound circles with conditional attributes on axes created by scaling functions
• Responsive labels that when selected will trigger transformations in the circles and axes relating to the different desired data
• Event listeners for changing dropdown or clicks on circles that filter individual store data

Engineering


• Isolating the circles by store number without filtering out the rest was challenging, this was solved by attributing ids to them based on their store number
• The sample size of only 45 stores over only 3 years from ten years ago

Issues & Limitations



• Visualize the strength of the relationship between weekly sales and the 4 factors as a whole and at individual stores
• Compare the performances of different stores under different conditions

Stock

Summary


• Moving Average was calculated for the window of 180 days in order to reflect differences of open and close prices of stock for each day
• Using plotly in Javascript to plot the stock candlestick.

Engineering


• Difficulty to show the volume in the plot, but with some modification in the layout, the volume was included in the plot
• Data was restricted to 10 years for faster loading

Issues & Limitations

• By choosing any of the selector options on the corner of the plot, it displays data proportionally for the first month, first 6 months, a year to date, final 1 year, and all the data
• Range selector option down the graph is an option to select the desired period of time
